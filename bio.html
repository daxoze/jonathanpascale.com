Jonathan Pascale grew up in Fort Worth, TX and graduated from North Crowley High School in 2012. After attending Tarrant County College, he now attends the University of Houston. He studies Mechanical Engineering Technology at UH, with a focus on computer-aided design and manufacturing.

Outside the classroom, Jonathan is also an active member of the Spirit of Houston. His interests include design and fabrication of innovative tehcnoligies in themed entertainment, in areas such as theme parks, museum exhibits, live shows, and other immersive experiences.

Jonathan previously worked at Trader Joe's (Fort Worth, TX) and Six Flags Hurricane Harbor (Arlington, TX).

Jonathan is 23 years old and lives in Houston, TX.
