Jonathan Pascale grew up in Fort Worth, TX and graduated from North Crowley High School in 2012. After attending Tarrant County College, he now attends the University of Houston. He studies Mechanical Engineering Technology at UH, with a focus on computer-aided design and manufacturing. His interests include design and fabrication of innovative technologies in themed entertainment, encompassing areas such as theme parks, museum exhibits, live shows, and other immersive experiences.

Outside the classroom, Jonathan is also an active member of the Spirit of Houston.

Jonathan currently works at Texas AirHogs Baseball (Grand Prairie, TX), and previously at Trader Joe's (Fort Worth, TX) and Six Flags Hurricane Harbor (Arlington, TX).

Jonathan is 24 years old and lives in Houston, TX.
